Welcome

We're glad you found us.

Lincoln Highway Baptist Church is a new congregation with old convictions — that the Bible is true, that Jesus Christ is Lord, and that the local church is where ordinary people are changed by an extraordinary God. We keep it simple: faithful preaching, heartfelt worship, and real community.

You don't need to dress up, know the Bible, or have it all figured out. Come as you are — we'd love to meet you.

What We Believe

We are an independent Baptist church holding to the historic Christian faith — the authority of Scripture, salvation by grace through faith in Christ alone, and a life lived for His glory.

The Scriptures

The Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God — our only complete and final authority.

The Godhead

One true and eternal God in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

Virgin-born, sinless, crucified for our sins, bodily risen, and coming again.

Salvation

A free gift of God's grace through faith in Christ alone — and the believer is eternally secure.

The Ordinances

Believer's baptism by immersion and the Lord's Supper, kept as a memorial.

His Return

Christ will return personally and bodily to reign — our blessed hope.

We share the doctrine of our sending church, First Baptist Church of Jackson. A full Statement of Faith will be published here.
