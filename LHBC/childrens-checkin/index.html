What happens on check-in

The kiosk writes a record, then (when connected) syncs it to the church management system.

1
Local write

A checkin row is inserted with child, guardian, room, timestamp, and who checked them in.

2
Secure pickup code

A short code is generated and printed on both the child tag and the guardian tag.

3
ChMS sync

The record posts to your ChMS (Planning Center Check-Ins, Breeze, ChurchTrac…) as an attendance event.

4
Confirmation & attendance

The ChMS returns an ID; the row is marked synced. Attendance persists for the dashboard & annual report.
