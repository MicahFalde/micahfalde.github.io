How a Sunday flows through

Paper in, clean named attendance out — review only the exceptions.

1
Usher section counts

Instant reliable totals — the backstop for "how many."

2
Scan the stack

Sheets run through the sheet-fed printer (ADF) into a watched OneDrive folder. The barcode tags each sheet's section + date.

3
Read the checkboxes (OMR)

Marks next to printed names are read at Scantron reliability — ~85% of attenders, zero handwriting needed.

4
Recognize the guest lines (OCR)

Only the handwritten lines are transcribed and matched against the member roster (closed set, not open text) — with a confidence score.

5
Route by confidence

High → saved automatically. Low → the review queue for a person.

6
Learn & promote

Confirmations raise a person's confidence; a confirmed guest is promoted onto next week's printed roster — graduating from handwriting to a checkbox.

7
Inference + weekly QA

Auto-mark anyone whose child checked in or who gave that morning. A weekly random sample is human-checked to catch drift.
