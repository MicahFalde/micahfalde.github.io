How a Sunday flows through

Many ways in, one attendance record out — review only the exceptions.

1
Three lanes, one record

Tap (NFC/QR), church app, or sign the sheet — all write to the same attendance database, tagged by lane.

2
Tap & app = instant + exact

No transcription, no review. A perfect record the moment someone checks in.

3
Handwriting → scan → read

Sheets scan into a watched folder; OCR transcribes each line and matches it against the member roster (closed set), with a confidence score.

4
Route by confidence

High → saved automatically. Low → the review queue for a person.

5
Learn

Each confirmation teaches the recognizer that person's handwriting, so next week it auto-clears. Regulars stop needing review.

6
Inference + usher counts

Auto-mark anyone whose child checked in or who gave; ushers give the totals backstop. Weekly random-sample QA catches drift.

7
Nudge the migration

Offer fobs/QR + the app; as adoption grows the handwriting pile shrinks — the system gets easier the longer it runs.
