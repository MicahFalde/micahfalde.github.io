Peach Torte

A sour-cream crust under a quart of fresh peaches.

In her hand, on a folded sheet.

Ingredients

  • 1¼ csifted flourcrust
  • ¼ tsaltcrust
  • ½ cbuttercrust
  • 2 Tsour creamcrust
  • 1 qtfresh sliced peachesfilling
  • 3egg yolks, beatentopping
  • ⅓ csour creamtopping
  • 1 csugartopping
  • ¼ call-purpose flourtopping
  • ¼ tsalttopping

Method

  1. For the crust, work the flour, salt, butter and sour cream together and press into a 9 × 9 pan. Bake at 375 °F for 20 minutes.
  2. Spread the sliced peaches over the crust.
  3. Mix the topping ingredients and pour over the peaches.
  4. Bake 35–40 minutes. Serve with ice cream or whipped cream.

As written

Peach Torte
Crust: 1¼ cups sifted flour · ¼ teas salt · ½ cup butter · 2 T sour cream
Filling: 1 qt. fresh sliced peaches
Topping: 3 egg yolks, beaten · ⅓ cup sour cream · 1 cup beet sugar · ¼ cup all-purpose flour · ¼ teas. salt

Press crust in 9×9 pan, bake 375°F 20 min. Spread on peaches. Mix other ingredients & pour over peaches. Bake 35–40 min. Serve with ice cream or whipped cream.
