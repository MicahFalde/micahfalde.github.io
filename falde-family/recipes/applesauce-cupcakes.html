Applesauce Cupcakes

Spiced, fruited, and finished with a coffee glaze.

In her hand, front and back.

Ingredients

  • ½ cbutter
  • 1 csugar
  • 1egg
  • 1 cthick unsweetened applesauce
  • 1¾ cflour
  • 1 craisins
  • 1 cnut meats, or currants
  • ¼ tsalt
  • 1 tsoda
  • 1 tcinnamon
  • ½ tcloves
  • 1 cpowdered sugarcoffee glaze
  • 4 tcold strong coffeecoffee glaze
  • —pecan halvesto finish

Method

  1. Beat the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Sift the flour and measure; sift a little over the raisins and nuts, and resift the rest with the salt, soda, cinnamon and cloves.
  2. Beat the egg into the butter and sugar, then gradually stir in the sifted ingredients, keeping the batter smooth. Add the raisins and nuts. Heat the applesauce and beat it in.
  3. Fill greased muffin pans half full; bake at 375 °F about 20 minutes. Makes 2 dozen medium cupcakes.
  4. When cool, spread with the coffee glaze (1 cup powdered sugar beaten with 4 t cold strong coffee) and set a pecan half on each.
