Kuchen

A sweet yeast dough under a Half-and-Half custard.

In her hand, on a folded sheet. She wrote the two ingredient lists and left the method unwritten — she knew it.

Method

Only the two ingredient lists are written down, headed “peaches / filling.” This is the Dakota-German custard Kuchen: make a soft sweet yeast dough from the first list, let it rise and press it into pans, lay on fruit (peaches), pour the custard from the second list over the top, and bake until the custard is just set. The method is our reading; the card gives none.
