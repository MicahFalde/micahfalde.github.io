Ripe Cucumber Pickles

Big ripe cucumbers, kept firm with a little alum.

In her hand, in blue ink that has bled in places.

Ingredients

  • —ripe cucumbers, peeled and cut in layers
  • —a few kernels of alum
  • 3 cvinegarsyrup
  • 1 cwatersyrup
  • 5 csugar (4 for apples)syrup
  • 1 Tspicessyrup

Method

  1. Peel the cucumbers and cut in layers. Salt the layers and let stand 2 hours; wash in clear water.
  2. Boil in water with a few kernels of alum (to keep them firm) until tender. Pour off the water and drain.
  3. Make the syrup: 3 cups vinegar, 1 cup water, 5 cups sugar and 1 tablespoon spices. Put in the cucumbers and boil. Seal in jars. Makes about 4 quarts.

She notes the same syrup takes 4 cups sugar “for apples.”
