Pickled Beets

Spiced with a thinly sliced lemon.

In her hand, on a much-spotted card.

Ingredients

  • —small beets, cooked and skinned
  • 2 csugar
  • 2 cwater
  • 3 cvinegar
  • 1lemon, sliced thin
  • 1 Tcinnamon
  • 1 tcloves
  • 1 tallspice

Method

  1. Cook small beets until tender. Cold-dip and slip the skins.
  2. Make a pickling syrup of the sugar, water, vinegar, sliced lemon, cinnamon, cloves and allspice.
  3. Cover the beets with the mixture and simmer 15 minutes. Seal in jars. Makes about 3 quarts.
