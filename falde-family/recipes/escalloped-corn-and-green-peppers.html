Escalloped Corn & Green Peppers

Corn baked in layers under crushed potato chips.

A printed recipe card she kept.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbutter
  • ¼ cchopped onion
  • ½ cchopped green pepper
  • 1 canwhole kernel white corn (No. 303)
  • ¼ ctop milk
  • ⅛ tpepper
  • ¼ tsalt
  • 2 ccrushed potato chips

Method

  1. Simmer the onion and green pepper in the butter slowly until tender but not brown.
  2. Add the corn and its liquid, the top milk and the seasonings.
  3. In a greased casserole, layer the corn mixture and crushed potato chips, repeating and ending with chips.
  4. Bake in a moderately hot oven (375 °F) about 25 minutes, until the top is crisp and brown. Serves 6.

As written

Escalloped Corn and Green Peppers
3 tablespoons butter · ¼ cup chopped onion · ½ cup chopped green pepper · No. 303 can Whole Kernel White Corn · ¼ cup top milk · ⅛ teaspoon pepper · ¼ teaspoon salt · 2 cups crushed potato chips

Simmer onion and green pepper in butter slowly until tender, but not brown. Add corn and liquid, top milk and seasonings. Place a layer of corn mixture in greased casserole, add a layer of crushed potato chips. Repeat until casserole is full, ending with potato chips. Bake in moderately hot oven (375°F) about 25 minutes or until top is crisp and brown. Yield 6 servings.
