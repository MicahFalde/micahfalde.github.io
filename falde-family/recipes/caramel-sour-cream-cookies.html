Caramel Sour Cream Cookies

A soft, nutmeg-scented sour-cream cookie.

A clipping she saved.

Ingredients

  • ½ cbutter
  • ⅔ cbrown sugar
  • 1egg, beaten
  • ½ csour cream
  • 2¼ csifted flour
  • ½ tsalt
  • 1½ tbaking powder
  • ¼ tsoda
  • ½ tnutmeg

Method

  1. Cream the butter, add the brown sugar and cream well. Beat the egg into the sour cream.
  2. Sift the flour, salt, baking powder, nutmeg and soda together, and add alternately with the egg-and-cream to the creamed butter and sugar. Chill thoroughly.
  3. The dough is very soft; mold it with a cooky press without chilling, or roll a small portion at a time, lightly, on a floured cloth, cut, and lift to the sheet with a spatula.
  4. Sprinkle with sugar and bake in a moderate oven (about 375 °F).

As written

Caramel Sour Cream Cookies
½ cup butter · ⅔ cup brown sugar · 1 egg, beaten · ½ cup sour cream · 2¼ cups sifted flour · ½ teaspoon salt · 1½ teaspoon combination baking powder · ¼ teaspoon soda · ½ teaspoon nutmeg

Cream butter. Add brown sugar, and cream well. Add beaten egg to sour cream. Mix and sift flour, salt, baking powder, nutmeg and soda together. Add alternately with the egg and cream mixture to the creamed butter and sugar. Chill thoroughly. The dough is very soft and may be molded without chilling with a cooky molder. If rolled, take a small portion at a time from the refrigerator, roll lightly and quickly on a floured cloth-covered board, cut and transfer to a cooky sheet with a spatula. Sprinkle with sugar. Bake in moderate oven.
