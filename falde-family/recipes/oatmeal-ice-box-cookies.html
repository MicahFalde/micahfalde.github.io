Oatmeal Ice Box Cookies

Shaped into rolls, chilled, and sliced as you need them.

Written in pencil on a sheet of pink floral stationery; the pencil has faded.

Ingredients

  • 1 cwhite sugar
  • 1 cbrown sugar
  • 1 cshortening
  • 2eggs
  • 1½ cflour, sifted with 1 t soda and 1 t salt
  • 1 tvanilla
  • 3 coatmeal
  • —nut meats, if desired

Method

  1. Cream the sugars and shortening well. Add the eggs.
  2. Add the sifted flour, soda and salt, then the vanilla, oatmeal and nuts.
  3. Shape into rolls and chill. Slice and bake.

The pencil is faint and the sifted-in amounts are our best reading. No oven temperature is given; an icebox cookie like this bakes around 375 °F until set.
