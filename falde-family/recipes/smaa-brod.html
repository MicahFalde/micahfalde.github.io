The card box

Smaa Brød

Rolled thin, fried on a griddle, cut round with a saucer.

Headed “from Ma Falde 4-19-55” and credited to Inga Beate Falde — the one card in the box that carries the family name. Re-copied 3-14-82.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cbutter or shortening
  • ½ tsalt
  • 1 tsugar, if desired
  • 1 ccream, sweet or sour
  • —flour, enough to roll out

Method

  1. Blend the butter, salt, sugar and cream. Stir in enough flour to roll out — try not to use too much.
  2. Roll out thin and fry in a frying pan or on a griddle.
  3. Cut out with the pattern of a saucer. Serve with butter and sprinkled with sugar.

This is the heart of the box: a Norwegian griddle bread in the Falde family’s own line, written down from Inga Beate “Ma” Falde in 1955 and copied out again in 1982. The unlabeled cream dough tucked loose in the box is almost the same recipe.
