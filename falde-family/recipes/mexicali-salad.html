Mexicali Salad

A taco salad stretched with oats — pure 1970s.

A printed “Spring-Fresh Recipes” card from Quaker Oats.

Ingredients

  • 2ripe avocados, peeled and cubedguacamole
  • 2 Tlemon juiceguacamole
  • 1 tsaltguacamole
  • 1 lbground beefsalad
  • ½ cchopped onionsalad
  • ¾ cQuaker Oats, uncookedsalad
  • 1 cspicy sweet French dressingsalad
  • 1 can (6 oz)tomato pastesalad
  • ½ cwatersalad
  • 1 can (7 oz)whole kernel cornsalad
  • 1 Tchili powdersalad
  • 1½ tsaltsalad
  • 5 cshredded lettucesalad
  • 2tomatoes, choppedsalad
  • 1 c (4 oz)shredded Cheddarsalad

Method

  1. For the guacamole, combine the avocado, lemon juice and salt; beat until well blended and chill.
  2. For the salad, brown the meat and onion; drain. Combine the oats, dressing, tomato paste, water, corn, chili powder and salt; add to the meat and heat thoroughly.
  3. Combine the lettuce and tomato. For each serving, place 1 to 1½ cups lettuce mixture on a plate; top with about ¾ cup meat mixture, cheese and guacamole.
  4. Garnish with corn chips if desired. Makes 6 servings.

As written

Mexicali Salad
Guacamole: 2 ripe avocados, peeled, cubed · 2 tablespoons lemon juice · 1 teaspoon salt
Salad: 1 lb. ground beef · ½ cup chopped onion · ¾ cup Quaker Oats (quick or old fashioned, uncooked) · 1 cup spicy sweet French dressing · One 6-oz. can tomato paste · ½ cup water · One 7-oz. can whole kernel corn · 1 tablespoon chili powder · 1½ teaspoons salt · 5 cups shredded lettuce · 2 tomatoes, chopped · 1 cup (4 oz.) shredded Cheddar cheese

For guacamole, combine avocado, lemon juice and salt. Beat until well blended; chill. For salad, brown meat and onion; drain. Combine oats, dressing, tomato paste, water, corn, chili powder and salt. Add to meat mixture; heat thoroughly. Combine lettuce and tomato. For each serving, place about 1 to 1½ cups lettuce mixture on plate. Top with about ¾ cup meat mixture, cheese and guacamole. Garnish with corn chips, if desired. Makes 6 servings.
