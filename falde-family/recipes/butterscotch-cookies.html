Butterscotch Cookies

A big brown-sugar drop cookie.

A clipping she saved; the very last words are lost where the paper is torn.

Ingredients

  • 2eggs
  • 1 tvanilla
  • ¼ cbutter
  • ¼ clard
  • 2 cbrown sugar
  • 3½ cflour
  • ½ teach soda, salt and cream of tartar

Method

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl with the vanilla. Melt the butter with an equal amount of lard.
  2. Beat the sugar into the eggs, then stir in the melted butter.
  3. Sift the flour with the dry ingredients and sift them into the batter a little at a time, beating after each addition.
  4. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto buttered cooky sheets and bake at 325 °F until lightly browned.

The clipping is torn at the last line — it ends “bake at 325 degrees until…”. “Until lightly browned” is our reading of the missing words.
