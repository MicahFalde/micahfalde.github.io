An Unlabeled Dough

No title, no name — a rolled dough on a torn scrap.

A pencil scrap tucked loose in the box, one of the two cards marked only “figure it out, Claude.”

Ingredients

  • 1 c+cream, or more
  • ½ clardor use all cream
  • ½ tsalt
  • ~3 cflour, enough to roll out

Method

  1. Beat the cream (or cream and lard) well with the salt.
  2. Stir in flour, about 3 cups, until the dough will just roll out.

Our best reading: this untitled scrap is a rolled dough, and almost certainly a plainer twin of the family’s Smaa Brød — same cream, salt and “flour enough to roll out.” Her own notes give the variations: “you can use all cream instead of lard,” and “you can use buttermilk & lard if you like.” The identification is ours; the card gives no title.

As written
