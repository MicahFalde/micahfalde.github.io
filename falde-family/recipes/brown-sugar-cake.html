Brown Sugar Cake

A crumb-topped snacking cake.

Credited on the card to Mrs. J., the same hand behind the Strawberry Jam Cake.

Ingredients

  • 2¼ cflour
  • 1½ cbrown sugar
  • ½ cwhite sugar
  • 2 tbaking powder
  • ½ cbutter
  • 1egg, well beaten
  • 1 csour milk
  • 1 tsoda
  • 2 tvanilla

Method

  1. Mix the flour, sugars and baking powder. Cut in the butter, and save out 1 cup of this mixture.
  2. Add the egg, sour milk and vanilla to the rest.
  3. Pour into a pan and sprinkle the reserved cup of crumbs on top.
  4. Bake ½ hour at 395–400 °F.
