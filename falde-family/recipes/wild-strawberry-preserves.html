Wild Strawberry
Preserves

“Wild berries are not washed.”

The most exacting card in the box: two pans, eight minutes, and a long wait to let the berries plump.

Ingredients

  • 4 chulled wild berries, unwashed
  • 2 csugar, for the berries
  • 2 csugar, for the drained juice

Method

  1. To 4 cups of hulled berries add 2 cups of sugar. Heat very slowly, without stirring, until plenty of juice is formed.
  2. Drain off the juice and pour it over 2 additional cups of sugar.
  3. Heat the juice and sugar in one pan, the berries in another. When both are boiling, combine and boil vigorously for 8 minutes.
  4. Remove from the fire and skim. Let stand 3 or 4 hours to plump, shaking occasionally.
  5. Reheat to boiling, then cool partially, to keep the berries from floating.
  6. Spoon the berries into jars, cover with the syrup, and seal.
