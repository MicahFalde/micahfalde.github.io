Strawberry Parfait Pie

Lemon Jell-O, vanilla ice cream, and sliced strawberries.

From a printed Pillsbury leaflet — Ann Pillsbury’s “Amazing Dessert Discovery,” promising 300 pies from one idea. One of four on the leaflet.

Ingredients

  • 1 pkgLemon Jell-O
  • 1¼ chot water
  • 1 pintvanilla ice cream
  • 1 cdrained sliced strawberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1baked 8-inch pie shell, cooled

Method

  1. Dissolve the Jell-O in the hot water in a 2-quart saucepan.
  2. Add the ice cream by spoonfuls, stirring until melted. Chill until thickened but not set (15 to 20 minutes).
  3. Fold in the berries, turn into the pie shell, and chill until firm (20 to 25 minutes).

As written

Strawberry Parfait Pie
1 package Lemon Jell-O · 1¼ cups hot water · 1 pint vanilla ice cream · 1 cup drained sliced strawberries (fresh or frozen) · 1 baked 8-inch Pillsbury pie shell, cooled

Dissolve Jell-O in hot water in 2-quart saucepan. Add ice cream by spoonfuls, stirring until melted. Then chill until thickened, but not set (15 to 20 minutes). Fold in berries. Turn into pie shell. Chill until firm (20 to 25 minutes).
