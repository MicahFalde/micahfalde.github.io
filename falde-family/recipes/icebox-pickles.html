The card box

Icebox Pickles

The card opens mid-thought, at “Quarter lengthwise.”

No cucumbers are listed and no quantity given. She knew how many she was starting with.

Ingredients

  • —cucumbers, quantity not given
  • 4 cvinegar
  • 2 csugar
  • ⅛ csaltpartly hidden under an ink blot

Method

  1. Quarter the cucumbers lengthwise. Let them stand in ice cold water 3 hours, or on ice.
  2. Have ready the syrup: boil the vinegar, sugar and salt together, then let it get cold.
  3. Pour over the pickles. Seal.

Because the card never says how many cucumbers, the syrup is the only fixed part. Four cups of vinegar makes enough for roughly a gallon jar packed tight.
