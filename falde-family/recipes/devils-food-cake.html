Devil’s Food Cake

A second Devil’s Food — a sour-milk layer cake, from Mrs. S.

Credited to Mrs. S. Not to be confused with her other Devil’s Food, the one from Ruth Eaton. The method finishes on the back with a single word: “layers.”

Ingredients

  • 2 cbrown sugar
  • ½ cfat (shortening)
  • 2eggs
  • ⅔ csour milk
  • ½ cboiling water
  • ⅓ cchocolate, melted
  • 1 tvanilla
  • 1 tsoda
  • ~3 cflour

Method

  1. Cream the sugar and fat; add the eggs whole, beating after each.
  2. Stir the soda into the sour milk and add.
  3. Melt the chocolate in the ½ cup boiling water. Sift in the flour and add the chocolate alternately. Stir in the vanilla.
  4. Bake in a moderate oven, in layers.

The ½ cup boiling water and the “½ c hot water” the method mentions are the same half-cup — used to melt the chocolate. No temperature or time is written; a moderate oven is about 350 °F, roughly 25–30 minutes for layers. That timing is our reading, not hers.
