Chocolate Drop Cookies

Chocolate drops with nuts and raisins — makes 25.

Filed under “Chocolate,” in her hand.

Ingredients

  • 1 csugar
  • ½ cCrisco
  • 2eggs, well beaten
  • 2 sqchocolate, or ⅓ cup cocoa
  • 1½ cpastry flour
  • ½ tvanilla
  • ¾ cnuts
  • ¾ craisins
  • ½ tsalt
  • ½ csweet milk + 2 t baking powder, or sour milk + soda

Method

  1. Cream the Crisco and sugar; add the eggs and the melted chocolate.
  2. Add the milk, vanilla, nuts and raisins and mix well.
  3. Add the flour and baking powder.
  4. Drop on a tin and bake (a moderate oven). Makes 25 cookies.
