Gingerbread

A soft molasses gingerbread.

Filed under the tab marked “Gingerbread,” headed “(home).”

Ingredients

  • ½ cbutter
  • ½ csugar
  • 1 cmolasses
  • ½ cmilk
  • 2 cflour
  • 2eggs
  • 1 tsoda
  • 1 tginger
  • 1 tsalt (scant)
  • 1 tcinnamon

Method

  1. Cream the butter, and add the sugar and molasses. Beat in the eggs.
  2. Add the dry ingredients (flour, soda, ginger, salt and cinnamon) alternately with the milk.
  3. Bake in a moderate oven (about 350 °F).
