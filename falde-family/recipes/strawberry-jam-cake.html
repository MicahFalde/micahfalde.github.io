Strawberry Jam Cake

Credited on the card to Mrs. J.

Two layers, filled with jam and covered in whipped cream. The last words run onto the back.

Ingredients

  • ½ lbsugarshe notes: 1¼ c.
  • ½ cshortening
  • ½ cbutter
  • 4eggs
  • ½ lbflour
  • 1 tbaking powder
  • ¼ tsalt
  • —strawberry jam, to fill
  • —whipped cream, to cover

Method

  1. Cream the sugar and shortening. Add the egg yolks one by one and beat.
  2. Add the flour.
  3. Bake in 2 layers.
  4. Fill with strawberry jam and cover with whipped cream.

The card lists 4 eggs but the method uses only the yolks. Either the whites were beaten and folded in, or they went into something else that afternoon. She does not say.
