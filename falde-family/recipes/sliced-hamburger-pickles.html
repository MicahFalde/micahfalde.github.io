Sliced & Hamburger Pickles

Two batches on one card — sweet slices, and garlicky dill chips.

In her hand, a recipe on each side.

Ingredients

  • 3 dozlarge cucumbers, slicedsliced pickles
  • 1 dozsmall onions, slicedsliced pickles
  • 1 qtvinegarsliced — syrup
  • —sugaramount lost under an ink blot
  • 1 ozcelery seedsliced — syrup
  • 3 twhite mustard seedsliced — syrup
  • 1 twhite peppersliced — syrup

Method

  1. Let the sliced cucumbers and onions stand a few hours with salt; drain. If too salty, soak in water.
  2. Boil the vinegar, sugar, celery seed, mustard seed and white pepper.
  3. Let the cucumbers stand in this mixture on the stove until heated through. Seal in jars.

The back holds a second recipe, Hamburger Pickles (about 6 quarts): slice 3 dozen large cucumbers into jars; add to each 1–2 cloves of garlic, a slice of onion and a head of dill. Boil 1 qt vinegar, 1 pt water, 2½ cups sugar and ½ cup salt, and pour over. The sugar amount in the sliced-pickle syrup is lost under an ink blot.
