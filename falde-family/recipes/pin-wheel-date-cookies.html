Pin-wheel Date Cookies

Rolled around a cooked date filling, then sliced into spirals.

In her hand, badly spotted with age, headed “3 doz.”

Ingredients

  • ⅓ clard
  • 1 cbrown sugar
  • 1egg, beaten
  • 1¾ cflour
  • 1½ tbaking powder
  • ¼ tsalt
  • ¾ cchopped datesfilling
  • ⅓ cwaterfilling
  • ¼ cwhite sugarfilling
  • 2 Tchopped nutsfilling

Method

  1. Cream the lard and brown sugar; add the beaten egg.
  2. Sift the flour, measure, and sift again with the baking powder and salt. Add to the creamed mixture and mix thoroughly. Roll ¼ inch thick.
  3. Cook the dates, water and white sugar over low heat five minutes; cool, then add the nuts.
  4. Spread the filling on the dough and roll it up. Chill, slice, and bake at 375 °F about 10 minutes. Makes 3 dozen.
