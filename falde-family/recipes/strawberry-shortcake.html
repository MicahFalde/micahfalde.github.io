Strawberry Shortcake

A rich biscuit, meant to be split and heaped with berries.

Credited on the card to Mrs. J.

Ingredients

  • 2 cflour
  • ½ tsalt
  • 4 tbaking powder
  • 2 Tsugar
  • 3 Tshortening
  • 1egg
  • ½ cmilk

Method

  1. Sift the dry ingredients and cut in the shortening.
  2. Beat the egg into the milk and add to the dry ingredients.
  3. Bake as a shortcake; split and fill with strawberries.

No oven temperature is given. A rich biscuit dough like this bakes in a hot oven, around 425–450 °F, until golden.
