The card box

Fatiman

Fattigmann — the Norwegian fried cardamom cookie, cut in diamonds.

In her hand, spelled “Fatiman” on the card, with a spilled ink blot across the middle.

Ingredients

  • 4eggs, beaten lightly
  • ¼ c (4 T)sugar
  • ¾ c (12 T)rich cream, whipped
  • —pinch of salt
  • ¼ tbaking powder
  • —ground cardamomamount smudged
  • ~3 cflour

Method

  1. Beat the eggs lightly with the sugar; fold in the whipped cream, salt, baking powder and cardamom.
  2. Work in about 3 cups of flour to a soft dough.
  3. Roll out and cut into diamonds with two slits in the center. Fry in deep lard.

“Fatiman” is her spelling of fattigmann — “poor man’s” cookies — a Norwegian Christmas fry. An ink blot covers the cardamom quantity.
