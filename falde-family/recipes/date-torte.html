Date Torte

Dates and black walnuts bound with bread crumbs — serves 8.

In her hand, in blue ink.

Ingredients

  • 3egg yolks, beaten
  • ¾ cdates, chopped
  • ½ cchopped black walnuts
  • 1 cbread crumbs
  • 1 csugar
  • ½ tsalt
  • 1 tvanilla
  • 3egg whites, beaten stiff

Method

  1. Mix the egg yolks, dates, nuts, bread crumbs, sugar and salt. Add the vanilla.
  2. Fold in the beaten whites.
  3. Turn into an 8 × 8 pan and bake at 350 °F for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve with whipped cream. Serves 8.
