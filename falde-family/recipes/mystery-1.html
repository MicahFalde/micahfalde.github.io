A Crystal Garden

Not food at all — a bowl that grows its own frost.

A slip tucked in the box, the other card marked only “figure it out, Claude.”

Ingredients

  • —a few pieces of coke (coal cinders)
  • ¼ teacupwater
  • ¼ teacupsalt
  • 5 Tbluing
  • —a little ammonia

What this is

This one is not a recipe — it is the old “crystal garden” (or “magic garden”). Pieces of coke or charcoal are set in a bowl and the mixed solution poured over them; as the water wicks up and evaporates, the salt and bluing grow into fragile, coral-like crystal blooms over a day or two. A Depression-era kitchen craft, filed in with the cakes. That’s our identification of the “figure it out” card.
