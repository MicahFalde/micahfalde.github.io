Chocolate Bits with Oatmeal

A hundred small chocolate-chip oatmeal cookies.

In her hand, front and back.

Ingredients

  • 1½ csifted flour
  • 1 tsoda
  • 1 tsalt
  • 1 cshortening
  • ¾ cbrown sugar
  • ¾ cwhite sugar
  • 2eggs, unbeaten
  • 1 thot water
  • 1 cnut meats, cut fine
  • 2 pkgchocolate bitspackage size unclear on the card
  • 2 coatmeal
  • 1 tvanilla

Method

  1. Sift the flour once before measuring, then sift with the soda and salt.
  2. Cream the shortening until soft. Add the sugars gradually, creaming until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating after each. Add the hot water, then the sifted dry ingredients.
  4. Add the nut meats, chocolate bits and oatmeal; mix thoroughly. Add the vanilla and blend well.
  5. Drop by half-teaspoons on a greased cookie sheet. Bake at 375 °F for 8 minutes. Makes 100 cookies.
