Ann Landers Meat Loaf

The meatloaf the whole country clipped in the 1960s.

In her hand, with the bake noted in the corner: 1 hour, 350°, serves six.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbground beef
  • 2eggs
  • 1½ cbread crumbs
  • ¾ cketchup
  • 1 tAccent (MSG seasoning)
  • ½ cwarm water
  • 1 pkgonion soup mix
  • 2 stripsbacon, if you like that flavor
  • 1 can (8 oz)tomato sauce

Method

  1. Mix the beef, eggs, bread crumbs, ketchup, Accent, warm water and onion soup mix thoroughly.
  2. Pack into a loaf pan. Cover with 2 strips of bacon if you like that flavor.
  3. Pour the can of tomato sauce over all.
  4. Bake 1 hour at 350 °F. Serves six.

This is the famous meat loaf Ann Landers reprinted in her column for decades — one of the most-clipped recipes in America. The card matches it, so it needs no filling-in.
