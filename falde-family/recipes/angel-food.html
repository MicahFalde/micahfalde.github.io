Angel Food

A twelve-white sponge, baked over a pan of boiling water.

Credited on the card to Mrs. S. The bake finishes on the back.

Ingredients

  • 12egg whites
  • 1 tvanilla
  • ½ tlemon extract
  • 1½ csugar
  • 1 c + 1 Tcake flour
  • 1½ tcream of tartar

Method

  1. Have everything measured before you begin. Sift the cream of tartar and flour together 3 or 4 times.
  2. Beat the egg whites until foamy, not stiff. Add the flavorings.
  3. Sift the sugar in slowly while stirring, then fold in the flour lightly.
  4. Bake in an ungreased tube pan 55–60 minutes, with a pan of boiling water under the cake for the first 45 minutes. Do not open the oven for the first 20 minutes.

No oven temperature is written; an angel food like this bakes at a moderate 350 °F.
