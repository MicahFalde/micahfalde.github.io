Sour Cream Chocolate Cake

A tender chocolate layer cake with a sour-cream orange icing.

A clipping she saved, with the matching icing clipping pasted beside it.

Ingredients

  • 2 ccake flour
  • 1 tsoda
  • ¼ tsalt
  • 2 sqbitter chocolate
  • ½ cshortening
  • 1½ csugar
  • 2eggs
  • ¾ csour cream
  • 1 tvanilla
  • ½ cboiling water or hot strong coffee

Method

  1. Sift the flour, measure, and resift 3 times with the soda and salt. Melt the chocolate over hot water and cool.
  2. Cream the shortening, adding the sugar gradually; add the whole eggs and beat until fluffy and light. Stir in the melted chocolate.
  3. Add the flour mixture in small portions alternately with the sour cream, stirring well after each. Add the vanilla, then stir in the boiling water or coffee.
  4. Turn into two buttered, waxed-paper-lined 8-inch tins and bake at 350 °F for 25–30 minutes (or 18 muffin tins at 400 °F for 20). Cool and spread with sour cream orange icing.

The pasted Sour Cream Orange Icing: cream 2 T butter, add 1¼ cups confectioners’ sugar gradually, moistening with ¼ cup sour cream as needed; stir in ½ t grated orange rind and beat until fluffy. Spread on the cooled cake and sprinkle with chocolate shot if you like.

As written

Sour Cream Chocolate Cake
2 cups cake flour · 1 teaspoon soda · ¼ teaspoon salt · 2 squares bitter chocolate · ½ cup shortening · 1½ cups sugar · 2 eggs · ¾ cup sour cream · 1 teaspoon vanilla · ½ cup boiling water or hot strong coffee

Sift flour, measure and resift 3 times with soda and salt. Melt chocolate over hot water; cool. Cream fat until plastic, adding sugar gradually; add whole eggs and beat until fluffy and light-colored. Stir in melted chocolate. Add flour mixture in small portions alternately with sour cream, stirring well after each addition. Add vanilla; then stir in the boiling water or coffee. Turn into two 8-inch cake tins buttered and lined with buttered waxed paper; bake in a moderate oven (350° F.) for 25 to 30 minutes. Or dip into 18 muffin tins and bake at 400° F. for 20 minutes. Cool and spread with Sour Cream Orange Icing.
Sour Cream Orange Icing — 2 tablespoons butter · 1¼ cups confectioners' sugar · ¼ cup sour cream · ½ teaspoon grated orange rind. Cream butter; add sugar gradually, moistening with cream as needed. Stir in rind and beat until fluffy. Spread between layers and on top and sides of cooled cake. Sprinkle if desired with chocolate shot.
