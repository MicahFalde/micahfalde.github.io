Chocolate Mahogany Cake

A cooked-chocolate layer cake.

Filed under “Chocolate,” headed “Mahogany cake (home).”

Ingredients

  • 1½ csugar
  • ½ cbutter
  • 1 csweet milk
  • ½ cgrated chocolate
  • 1 tsoda
  • 2eggs
  • 1 tvanilla
  • 2 cflour

Method

  1. Cream the butter and sugar; add ½ cup of the milk and the well-beaten eggs.
  2. Mix the flour and soda and add.
  3. Cook the grated chocolate in the rest of the milk until thick; cool, and stir into the batter last. Add the vanilla.
  4. Bake in two layers (a moderate oven, about 350 °F).
