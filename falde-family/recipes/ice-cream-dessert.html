Ice Cream Dessert

The newest card in the box: ballpoint pen, and a box of Rice Krispies.

On the card she brackets the first three together and writes “toast in broiler” beside them.

Ingredients

  • 2 ccrushed Rice Krispiesbraced together: “toast in broiler”
  • ½ ccoconut
  • ½ cchopped almonds
  • ½ cbrown sugar
  • ⅓ cmelted butter
  • 1 qtbutter brickle ice cream, or any flavour

Method

  1. Toast the Rice Krispies, coconut and almonds under the broiler.
  2. Blend all of the above together.
  3. Spread half the mixture over the bottom of a 9 × 9 pan and press firmly.
  4. Cover with the ice cream.
  5. Sprinkle the remainder over the top and freeze.

As written
