Carrot Cake

An oil cake heavy with carrots, coconut and pineapple.

In her hand, in blue ink. The bake is noted at the bottom.

Ingredients

  • 1½ ccooking oil
  • 2 csugar
  • 2¼ cflour
  • 2 tsoda
  • 1 tsalt
  • 4eggs
  • 2 tcinnamon
  • 2 tvanilla
  • 2 cshredded carrots
  • 2 cflaked coconut
  • ½–1 cnuts
  • 1 cancrushed pineapple

Method

  1. Beat the oil and sugar together; add the eggs one at a time.
  2. Add the sifted flour, soda, salt and cinnamon, then the vanilla.
  3. Stir in the carrots, coconut, nuts and pineapple.
  4. Pour into a 9 × 13 pan and bake at 350 °F for 40 minutes to 1 hour.

The card lists the ingredients, the pan and “350° for 40 min. to 1 hr.” The mixing order above is the usual one for an oil carrot cake; the card doesn’t spell it out.
