Fruit Cookies

A pound of candied fruit, so almost certainly a Christmas cookie.

Written in pencil, which has faded more than the ink cards around it.

Ingredients

  • 1 cbrown sugar
  • 1 cbutter
  • 1 cwhite sugar
  • 3large eggs, beaten well
  • 1 tsoda, in 2 T sweet milk
  • 3¾ cflour
  • 2 tbaking powder
  • 2 tvanilla
  • 1 lbmixed candied fruit
  • —nuts, if desired

Method

  1. Chill the dough in the ice box.
  2. Form into balls and press down. Sprinkle with sugar.
  3. Bake at 400 °F.

She gives no baking time. Cookies this size usually want 8 to 10 minutes at that heat, but that is our guess, not hers.
