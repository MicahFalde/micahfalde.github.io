Sour Cream
Spice Cakes

Twenty cup cakes, iced with hot brown sugar and cream.

Filed in the box under the tab marked “Spice cake”.

Ingredients

  • 1½ csifted flour
  • ½ tsoda
  • ½ tsalt
  • 1 tcinnamon
  • ½ tcloves
  • 2eggs
  • 1 cbrown sugar
  • 1 cthick sour cream

Method

  1. In a bowl, blend the eggs, sour cream and sugar.
  2. Add the sifted dry ingredients. Beat until smooth.
  3. Drop into muffin tins and bake in a moderate oven (375 °F) for 25 minutes. Makes 20 cup cakes.

Icing

  1. Heat to scalding 4 T brown sugar and 4 T cream.
  2. Add ½ t vanilla and powdered sugar, and spread.

She gives no quantity for the powdered sugar. Add it to the hot cream until the icing will just spread. It sets fast, so ice the cakes while they are still warm.
