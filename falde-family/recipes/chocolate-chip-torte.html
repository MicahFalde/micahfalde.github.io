Chocolate Chip Torte

A date cake, chocolate chips and nuts strewn over the top.

In her hand, filling the card.

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cflour
  • 2 Tcocoa
  • ½ tsalt
  • 1 tsoda
  • 1 cboiling water
  • 1 cdates, cut fine
  • 1 cshortening
  • 1 csugar
  • 2eggs, beaten
  • 1 tvanilla
  • 1 pkgchocolate chipspackage size smudged
  • ¾ cchopped nuts

Method

  1. Sift the flour, cocoa, salt and soda.
  2. Pour the boiling water over the dates cut fine; let stand.
  3. Cream the shortening and sugar; add the beaten eggs and vanilla.
  4. Add the dates and their water alternately with the dry ingredients.
  5. Spread in a 13 × 9 pan. Sprinkle the chocolate chips and nuts over the top.
  6. Bake at 350 °F for 45 minutes. Top with whipped cream.
