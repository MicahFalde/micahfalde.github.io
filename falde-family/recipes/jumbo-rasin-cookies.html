Jumbo Raisin Cookies

Soft, puffy, and full of boiled raisins.

A clipping she saved — a Sun-Maid and Spry advertisement, gone deep brown with age.

Ingredients

  • 2 cSun-Maid seedless raisins
  • 1 cwater
  • 1 tsoda
  • 1 cSpry (shortening)
  • 2 tsalt
  • ¼ tnutmeg
  • ¼ tallspice
  • 1½ tcinnamon
  • 2 csugar
  • 3eggs, beaten
  • 1 tvanilla
  • 4 csifted flour
  • 1 tbaking powder
  • 1 cchopped nuts

Method

  1. Boil the raisins and water 5 minutes (there should be ½ cup liquid left). Cool, then add the soda.
  2. Blend the Spry with the salt, spices and sugar until fluffy. Add the eggs and vanilla.
  3. Add the cooled raisin mixture, then the sifted dry ingredients and the nuts. Chill.
  4. Drop by spoonfuls on a greased and floured baking sheet. Bake in a fairly hot oven (400 °F) 12–15 minutes. Makes 6 dozen.

As written

Jumbo Raisin Cookies
2 cups Sun-Maid seedless raisins · 1 cup water · 1 tsp. soda · ¼ tsp. nutmeg · ¼ tsp. allspice · 2 cups sugar · 3 eggs, beaten · 1 tsp. vanilla · 1 cup Spry · 4 cups sifted flour · 2 tsps. salt · 1½ tsps. cinnamon · 1 tsp. baking powder · 1 cup chopped nuts

Boil raisins and water 5 mins. (There should be ½ cup liquid left.) Cool, add soda. Blend Spry with salt, spices and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs, vanilla. Add cooled raisin mixture. Add sifted dry ingredients and nuts. Chill. Drop by spoonfuls on greased and floured baking sheet, and bake in fairly hot oven (400°) 12–15 mins. Makes 6 dozen soft, puffy cookies.
