Red Devil’s Food

Her “large cake” version of the Devil’s Food.

On a soft, folded sheet that has faded almost to nothing; read here with the contrast pushed hard. The card gives ingredients only.

Ingredients

  • 2 csugar
  • ½ cbutter
  • 2eggs, well beaten
  • 1 csour milk, or half cream
  • ½ ccocoa, dissolved in enough hot water
  • ½ tsoda, heaping — dissolved in a little hot water
  • 1 tvanilla
  • ¼ tsalt
  • 2½ cflour

Method

The card is only an ingredient list, and a badly faded one. No method or oven temperature survives. Her smaller Devil’s Food in the same box suggests the way of it: cream the butter and sugar, beat in the eggs, then add the sour milk, the dissolved cocoa, the dry ingredients and the dissolved soda, and bake in a moderate oven. That is our reading, not hers.
