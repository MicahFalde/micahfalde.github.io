Prunella Cake

A spiced sour-milk cake built on stewed prunes.

Typed on the card, in her file.

Ingredients

  • ½ cCrisco
  • 1 csugar
  • 2eggs
  • ⅔ csour milk
  • 1⅓ cflour
  • ⅔ cstewed prunes
  • ½ teach soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice
  • —baking powder

Method

  1. Blend the Crisco with the sugar and eggs. Add the chopped prunes.
  2. Stir in the sour milk. Add the sifted dry ingredients.
  3. Pour into 2 Criscoed layer pans and bake in a moderate oven about 25 minutes.
  4. Cool, and use a creamy icing.

As written

Prunella Cake
½ c. Crisco · 1 c. sugar · 2 eggs · ⅔ c. sour milk · 1-1/3 c. flour · 2/3 c. stewed prunes · ½ t. each of soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice · baking powder

Blend Crisco with sugar and eggs. Add chopped prunes. Stir in milk. Add sifted dry ingredients. Pour into 2 criscoed layer pans. Bake in moderate oven about 25 minutes. Cool. Use Creamy Icing.
