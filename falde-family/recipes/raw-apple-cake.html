The card box

Raw Apple Cake

Sour milk or coffee, whichever was on the counter.

Written in pencil as a list of quantities, with no method at all.

Ingredients

  • 3 cflour
  • 1 cbrown sugar
  • 1 cwhite sugar
  • 1 cshortening
  • 2 capples, chopped
  • 2eggs
  • 2 tcinnamonbraced with the nutmeg as “spice”
  • 1 tnutmeg
  • 1 tsalt
  • 1½ tsoda
  • 1 craisins
  • 1 csour milk or coffee
  • 1 cnuts

Method

  1. Bake at 350 °F for 1 hour.

That single line is the whole method she wrote down. For a cake of this kind you would cream the shortening and sugars, beat in the eggs, add the dry ingredients alternately with the sour milk or coffee, then fold in the apples, raisins and nuts. That is our reading, not hers.
