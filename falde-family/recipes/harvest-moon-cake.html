Harvest Moon Cake

A brown-sugar spice cake under a fluffy brown-sugar frosting.

Credited to Mrs. S. The cake takes 2 whole eggs and 2 yolks; the 2 whites left over go into the frosting on the back — nothing wasted.

Ingredients

  • 1 cfat
  • 2 cbrown sugar
  • 2 + 2whole eggs, plus 2 yolks
  • 2½ cflour
  • 1 tcloves
  • 1 tcinnamon
  • 1 tsoda
  • ½ tsalt
  • 1 csour milk
  • 1 tbaking powder
  • 2egg whitesfrosting
  • 1 cbrown sugarfrosting

Method

  1. Cream the fat and add the sugar. Add the eggs separately, beating well each time.
  2. Sift in the flour, soda, baking powder, salt and spices alternately with the sour milk.
  3. Bake 45 minutes at 350 °F.
  4. For the frosting, beat the 2 egg whites and 1 cup brown sugar together over boiling water until it holds soft peaks, and spread on the cooled cake.

The card lists only “2 egg whites, 1 c brown sugar” for the frosting. That is a brown-sugar seven-minute frosting: beat the whites and sugar (with a spoonful of water and a pinch of salt) in the top of a double boiler over boiling water, about 7 minutes, until it stands in peaks. The method is the standard one for this frosting; the card gives only the two ingredients.
