Norwegian Bread

Julekake — a cardamom Christmas loaf, fruited and glazed.

In her hand, front and back, in blue ink.

Ingredients

  • 2 cmilk
  • 1 cmelted butter
  • 2 tsalt
  • 1 csugar
  • 2 pkgyeast
  • ½ clukewarm water
  • 8 cflour
  • 1 Tcardamom seeds
  • ½ cchopped almonds
  • 1 pkg (3 oz)candied cherries, sliced
  • 1 pkg (3 oz)citron, chopped
  • 1 cchopped raisins

Method

  1. Scald the milk; add the melted butter, salt and sugar. When lukewarm, stir in the yeast dissolved in the warm water.
  2. Stir in 4 cups of flour. Cover and let rise in a warm place about 2 hours, until doubled.
  3. Punch down and stir in the cardamom, almonds, cherries, citron and raisins. Work in the rest of the flour until the dough is as soft as can be handled.
  4. Cover and let rise 2 hours, until doubled. Knead slightly and place in two greased loaf pans; let rise until doubled.
  5. Bake in a moderate oven (350 °F) one hour. When cool, glaze with a thin confectioner’s-sugar icing.
