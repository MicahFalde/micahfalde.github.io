Banana Cake

Layers filled with whipped cream and sliced banana.

In her hand; the method finishes on the back.

Ingredients

  • ½ cshortening
  • 1½ csugar
  • 2eggs
  • 2 cflour
  • ½ tbaking powder
  • ¾ tsoda
  • ½ tsalt
  • ¼ csour milk
  • 1 tvanilla
  • 1 cmashed bananas (2 or 3)
  • 1 ccream, whipped and sweetenedfilling
  • 2bananas, slicedfilling

Method

  1. Cream the shortening and add the sugar gradually. Add the well-beaten eggs.
  2. Sift the flour once before measuring, then sift with the baking powder, soda and salt. Add alternately with the sour milk and bananas. Add the vanilla.
  3. Bake in layers.
  4. Put together with the whipped cream and sliced bananas.

No oven temperature is given; a butter layer cake like this bakes around 350 °F, 25–30 minutes.
