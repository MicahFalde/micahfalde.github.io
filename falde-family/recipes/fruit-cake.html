Fruit Cake

A dark applesauce fruitcake — pounds of it, for Christmas.

Credited on the card to Mrs. S. The “#” marks are pounds.

Ingredients

  • 2eggs
  • 2 capplesauce
  • 1 teach cloves, cinnamon, allspice & soda
  • 2 cbrown sugar
  • ½ tsalt
  • 4 cflour
  • 1 lbcurrants
  • 2 cCrisco
  • 1 lbdates
  • 1½ lbseeded raisins
  • ½ lbmixed peel
  • —nut meats

Method

  1. Cream the sugar and shortening and add the eggs, unbeaten.
  2. Then add everything else.
  3. Bake in a slow oven (about 300 °F) 3 hours.

As written
