Lard Cake

A thrifty everyday cake — and the base for three others.

Water-damaged: the ink washed through the middle of the front. The amounts and method below are reconstructed by cross-reading the bleed-through, the back, and the recipe’s own arithmetic; anything uncertain is flagged.

Ingredients

  • ¾ clard
  • 1½ csugar
  • 1 tsalt
  • 1½ tvanilla
  • 3eggs
  • 1 cmilk or water
  • 3 cflour
  • 3 tbaking powder

Method

  1. Cream the lard, 1 cup of the sugar, the salt and 2 tablespoons of the milk until light and fluffy. Add the vanilla.
  2. Separate the eggs. Drop the yolks into the milk and stir thoroughly. Add the milk-and-yolks alternately with the sifted flour and baking powder.
  3. Beat the whites stiff, beating in the remaining ½ cup sugar, and fold into the batter.
  4. Bake in layers or a loaf at 350 °F, about 30 minutes (the exact figure is washed out; layers run ~25–30 minutes, a loaf longer).

This is the wet card. The reconstruction rests on solid ground: the method itself accounts for the sugar (“1 c.” creamed in, “remaining ½ c.” beaten into the whites — the 1½ cups listed), and the back, which survived clearly, confirms the 350 ° bake and gives three variations off this same batter — White cake (4 egg whites instead of the whole eggs, a few drops of almond), Gold cake (4–5 yolks, add 2 t orange or lemon rind), and Mocha Spice cake (blend 1 t each cinnamon, nutmeg and allspice with ½ c strong hot coffee, cooled, plus ½ c water or milk, as the liquid). The one figure we can’t recover is the exact bake time.

As written

Lard Cake
¾ c. lard · 1½ c. sugar · 1 t. salt · 1½ t. vanilla · 3 eggs · 1 c. milk or water · 3 c. flour · 3 t. baking powder

Cream lard, 1 c. sugar, salt and 2 T of milk until light and fluffy. Add vanilla. Separate eggs, drop yolks into milk and stir thoroughly. Add milk and egg yolks alternately with sifted flour and B.P. Beat whites stiff, [beat] in remaining ½ c. sugar and fold into batter.

Bake in layers or loaf (350°) for [~30?] min.
Variations —
White cake: use 4 egg whites instead of 3 eggs. All as above. Add few drops almond.
Gold cake: use 4 or 5 egg yolks. Beat until light and add with ½ c. sugar last. Add 2 t. orange or lemon rind.
Mocha Spice cake: blend 1 t. cinnamon, 1 t. nutmeg, 1 t. allspice with ½ c. strong black hot coffee. Cool. Add ½ c. water or milk and use as liquid.
