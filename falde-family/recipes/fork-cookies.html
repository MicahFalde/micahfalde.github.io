Fork Cookies

Clipped from Successful Farming and copied out in her hand.

Filed in the box under the tab marked “Fork”. Written on both sides of the card.

Ingredients

  • 1 clard
  • 1 cbrown sugar
  • 1 cwhite sugar
  • 3eggs, well beatenwritten over a struck-out 2
  • 3½ cflour
  • 1 tsodashe struck out a 2 and wrote 1
  • 2 tcream tartar
  • ½ tsalt
  • 1 tvanilla

Method

  1. Cream the shortening and sugars. Add the eggs; mix well.
  2. Sift the flour, measure, then sift again with the soda, cream of tartar and salt. Add to the first mixture.
  3. Add the vanilla; mix well. Chill for an hour or two.
  4. Pinch off dough and shape into balls. Place on a cookie sheet and press flat with a floured fork.
  5. Bake in a hot oven (400 °F) 12 to 15 minutes. Makes 5 dozen.
