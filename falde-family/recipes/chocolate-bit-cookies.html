Chocolate Bit Cookies

Her Toll House — a box of chocolate bits, or raisins instead.

In her hand; the method finishes on the back.

Ingredients

  • ½ cbutter
  • ¼ cwhite sugar
  • ½ cbrown sugar
  • 1egg
  • 1 thot water
  • ½ cnuts, if desired
  • 1 c + 2 Tflour
  • ½ tsoda
  • ½ tsalt
  • 1 boxchocolate bits, or raisins
  • ½ tvanilla

Method

  1. Soften the butter and beat until creamy; add the two sugars. Add the well-beaten egg and the hot water, and beat again.
  2. Sift the flour with the salt and soda and add.
  3. Add the nuts, chocolate bits and vanilla.
  4. Drop by teaspoonfuls and bake in a moderately hot oven (about 375 °F) 10–12 minutes.

The last line of the method runs onto the back of the card — the usual drop-and-bake finish for this Toll House-style cookie. No temperature is written; 375 °F is the standard.
