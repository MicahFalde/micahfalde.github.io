Snow Cake

A winter cake leavened with a cup of new-fallen snow.

In her hand. The snow goes in last, for lightness — an old winter trick.

Ingredients

  • 1 csugar
  • ½ cshortening
  • ½ tsalt
  • 1 cmilk
  • —vanilla
  • 2 tbaking powder
  • 2 cflour
  • 1 cnew-fallen snowadded last

Method

  1. Cream the sugar and shortening with the salt.
  2. Add the milk, vanilla, baking powder and flour.
  3. Last, fold in 1 cup of new-fallen snow.
  4. Bake in a moderate oven, in layers or a loaf.

Folding in fresh snow at the end was a real winter shortcut: the trapped air (and, in some tellings, a trace of ammonia in the snow) lightened the cake in place of extra eggs. A moderate oven is about 350 °F.
