Apple Suzettes

Apple pancakes rolled around a warm apple-molasses filling.

In her hand, front and back. Serves 6.

Ingredients

  • 2eggs, beaten
  • ¾ cmilk
  • 3 Tmelted shortening
  • ½ cfinely chopped apple
  • 1 cflour
  • 1 tsalt
  • 3 tbaking powder
  • 1 Tsugar
  • 3½ cfinely chopped applefilling
  • ½ cmolassesfilling

Method

  1. Combine the eggs, milk and shortening. Add the ½ cup chopped apple and the sifted dry ingredients. Beat.
  2. Bake on an ungreased griddle, like pancakes.
  3. For the filling, cook the 3½ cups apples with the molasses until tender.
  4. Place a spoonful of filling on each suzette, roll it up, and sprinkle with sugar. Serves 6.

As written

Apple Suzettes
2 beaten eggs · ¾ c. milk · 3 T. melted shortening · ½ c. finely chopped apple · 1 c. flour · 1 teas. salt · 3 teas. baking powder · 1 T sugar
Filling: 3½ c. finely chopped apple & ½ c. molasses

Combine eggs, milk, and shortening. Add ½ c. chopped apple; and sifted dry ingredients. Beat. Bake on ungreased griddle. Filling — Cook 3½ c. apples with molasses until tender. Place spoonful of filling on each suzette. Roll. Sprinkle with sugar. Serves 6.
