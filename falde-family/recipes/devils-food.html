Devil’s Food

“Good to use up sour cream on hand.”

Written on the card in her hand, credited in the corner to Ruth Eaton.

Method

The card gives no method; she did not need one written down. Going by the Sour Cream Spice Cakes card in the same box, her way was: blend the egg, sour cream and sugar in a bowl, add the sifted dry ingredients, beat until smooth, then bake in a moderate oven (375 °F) until it springs back: about 25 minutes for cup cakes, longer for a layer. This is our best reading, not her words. If anyone remembers how she actually baked it, that belongs here instead.
