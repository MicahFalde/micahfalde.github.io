Merry Mint Brownies

A Christmas brownie under a green peppermint icing.

A clipping she saved — a Spry shortening advertisement, with a photograph of the green-iced brownies.

Ingredients

  • ¾ c + 2 Tsifted cake flour
  • 1 csugar
  • 7 Tcocoa
  • ½ tbaking powder
  • ¾ tsalt
  • ⅔ cSpry (shortening)
  • 2eggs, unbeaten
  • 1 tvanilla
  • 1 Tcorn syrup
  • 1 cwalnuts, coarsely chopped

Method

  1. Sift the first five ingredients into a mixing bowl. Add the Spry, eggs, vanilla and corn syrup.
  2. Mix thoroughly — 200 round-the-bowl strokes, or 2 minutes on a mixer at low speed. Stir in the walnuts.
  3. Bake in a greased 8 × 8 × 2 pan in a moderate oven (350 °F) 40–45 minutes. Cool and remove from the pan.
  4. For the Mint-O-Green frosting: melt 1 T each Spry and butter in 2½ T scalded light cream. Pour over 2 cups sifted confectioners’ sugar and ¼ t salt; stir until dissolved. Add ½ t vanilla and ¼ t peppermint extract; beat until thick enough to spread, and tint Christmas green.
  5. Spread over the brownies. Melt 1 oz. chocolate with 1 t Spry, cool, and drizzle across the frosting in a stream.

As written

Here's How to Make Merry Mint Brownies
¾ cup plus 2 tablespoons sifted cake flour · 1 cup sugar · 7 tablespoons cocoa · ½ teaspoon baking powder · ¾ teaspoon salt · ⅔ cup Spry · 2 eggs, unbeaten · 1 teaspoon vanilla · 1 tablespoon corn sirup · 1 cup walnuts, coarsely chopped

Sift first 5 ingredients into mixing bowl. Add Spry, eggs, vanilla and corn sirup. Mix thoroughly by beating 200 round-the-bowl strokes (2 minutes on mixer at low speed). Stir in walnuts. Bake in Spry-coated 8″ x 8″ x 2″ pan in moderate oven (350°F.) 40–45 minutes. Cool, remove from pan, and spread Mint-O-Green Frosting on top.
Mint-O-Green Frosting: Melt 1 tablespoon each Spry and butter in 2½ tablespoons scalded light cream. Pour over 2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar and ¼ teaspoon salt; stir until sugar is dissolved. Add ½ teaspoon vanilla and ¼ teaspoon peppermint extract; beat until thick enough to spread. Add enough green food coloring to tint a Christmas green. Melt 1 oz. chocolate and 1 teaspoon Spry together, cool, and drizzle from teaspoon in continuous stream across frosting.
