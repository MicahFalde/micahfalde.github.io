Brown Cookies

A rolled molasses cookie, spiced by the handful.

Filed under the tab marked “Molasses.” Written over an older “Brown Cookies.”

Ingredients

  • 2 tsoda
  • 1 csour cream
  • 1 cshortening
  • 2eggs
  • 2 csugar
  • 1 cmolasses
  • ½ tnutmeg
  • —cloves, allspice
  • —salt
  • —enough flour to roll them out

Method

  1. Combine all the ingredients, adding enough flour to make a dough stiff enough to roll.
  2. Roll out and cut. Bake.

She gives no amount for the cloves, allspice or salt, no flour quantity, and no oven temperature. Her other rolled molasses cookies go into a moderate oven (around 350–375 °F) for 8–10 minutes.
