Banana Parfait Pie

Strawberry Jell-O, vanilla ice cream, and sliced bananas.

From a printed Pillsbury leaflet — Ann Pillsbury’s “Amazing Dessert Discovery,” promising 300 pies from one idea. One of four on the leaflet.

Ingredients

  • 1 pkgStrawberry Jell-O
  • 1¼ chot water
  • 1 pintvanilla ice cream
  • 1½ csliced bananas
  • 1baked 9-inch pie shell, cooled

Method

  1. Dissolve the Jell-O in the hot water in a 2-quart saucepan.
  2. Add the ice cream by spoonfuls, stirring until melted. Chill until thickened but not set (15 to 20 minutes).
  3. Fold in the bananas, turn into the pie shell, and chill until firm (10 to 15 minutes).

As written

Banana Parfait Pie
1 package Strawberry Jell-O · 1¼ cups hot water · 1 pint vanilla ice cream · 1½ cups sliced bananas · 1 baked 9-inch Pillsbury pie shell, cooled

Dissolve Jell-O in hot water in 2-quart saucepan. Add ice cream by spoonfuls, stirring until melted. Then chill until thickened, but not set (15 to 20 minutes). Fold in bananas. Turn into pie shell. Chill until firm (10 to 15 minutes).
