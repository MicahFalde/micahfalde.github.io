Pop Corn Balls

Or Cracker Jacks — maple, sorghum and sugar, boiled hard.

Credited on the card to Mildred.

Ingredients

  • 1 cmaple syrup
  • 1 csorghum
  • 1 csugar
  • —popped corn

Method

  1. Boil the syrup, sorghum and sugar until a little hardens dropped in water.
  2. Beat until cool and pour over the popped corn.
  3. Mix in the corn well and form into balls.

As written

Pop corn balls or Cracker Jacks (Mildred)
1 c maple sirup · 1 c sorghum · 1 c sugar

Boil until it hardens in water. Beat until cool and pour over corn. Mix in corn well and form balls.
