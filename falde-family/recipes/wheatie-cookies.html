Wheatie Cookies

Balls the size of walnuts, pressed down with a fork.

Filed under the tab marked “Wheatie”. A spilled ink blot has taken part of the card.

Ingredients

  • 1 cwhite sugar
  • 1 cbrown sugar
  • 2 cWheaties
  • 2 ccoconut
  • 1 cshortening
  • 1 tbaking powder
  • 1 tsoda
  • 3 cflourpartly under the ink blot
  • 2eggs, beatenpartly under the ink blot

Method

  1. Cream the shortening, sugar and eggs.
  2. Add the other ingredients and mix all together.
  3. Form balls the size of walnuts, then press down with a fork in the pan and bake.

No oven temperature and no time. Her other drop cookies go into a hot oven at 400 °F for 10 to 12 minutes, which is where we would start.
