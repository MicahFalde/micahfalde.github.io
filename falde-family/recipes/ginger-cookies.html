Grandmother’s
Ginger Cookies

Her grandmother’s, which puts this recipe another generation back again.

Filed under the tab marked “Ginger cookies”.

Ingredients

  • 1 cbrown sugar
  • 1 cmolasses
  • ½ cCrisco
  • 1egg, beaten
  • 1 tginger
  • 1 tsalt
  • 1 tsoda
  • 1 csour milk
  • 4½ cpastry flour

Method

  1. Cream the Crisco, sugar and salt. Add the molasses and ginger. Add the egg.
  2. Dissolve the soda in the sour milk and add.
  3. Add the flour. Drop on a hot pan and bake. Makes 50 cookies.

No oven temperature is given. A moderate oven, around 350 °F, suits a molasses drop cookie, but the card does not say so.
