Tomato Soup

Put up by the peck, to open on a winter day.

In her hand, a canning recipe on lined paper.

Ingredients

  • 14 lbs (1 peck)tomatoes
  • 1 bunchcelery
  • 4onions
  • 1green pepper
  • 1 csugar
  • 3 Tsalt
  • ½ cbutter
  • 1 cflour

Method

  1. Boil the tomatoes, celery, onions and green pepper together and strain.
  2. Add the sugar and salt.
  3. Melt the butter and flour together; add a little of the liquid to this paste gradually, then combine with the rest.
  4. Boil 5 minutes and seal. Makes 9 or 10 pints.

As written

Tomato Soup
14 lbs. or 1 peck tomatoes · 1 bunch celery · 4 onions · 1 green pepper

Boil together and strain. Add: 1 c. sugar, 3 tblsp. salt. Melt ½ c. butter and 1 c. flour together. Add a little liquid to this paste gradually. Boil 5 minutes and seal. Makes 9 or 10 pints.
