Sweet Cherry Conserve

Cherries and walnuts, set soft with Certo.

In her hand, front and back, with a note in the margin: “I used a little grated lemon rind too.”

Ingredients

  • 3¾ cpitted cherries
  • 7 csugar
  • ¼ clemon juice
  • 1 cchopped walnuts, not fine
  • 1 bottleCerto
  • —a little grated lemon rind (her note)

Method

  1. Bring the fruit and sugar to a full rolling boil. Boil 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  2. Remove from the heat and stir in the Certo at once. Skim.
  3. Then stir and skim for 5 minutes to cool, and ladle into jars.

As written
