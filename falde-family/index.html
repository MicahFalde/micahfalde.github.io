Grandma Falde’s recipe cards, scanned exactly as they are: stains, pencil second-guesses and all. Each one is typed out beside the scan, so we can actually cook from them.

The card on top of the box: Fork Cookies

Adding to this

This is a keeping place, not a finished thing. If you have a card, a photograph of one, or the story that goes with a recipe (who made it, who it was for, the year it went wrong), send it along and it gets added here. Stories will live beside the recipes once there are some to tell.

A photograph taken on a phone is plenty. The cards are small and the writing is faint, so more light beats more megapixels.
