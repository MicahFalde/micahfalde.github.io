The Falde Family

In her
own hand

Grandma Falde’s recipe box, scanned exactly as it is: her own cards, the ones she copied from friends, and the clippings she saved, stains and pencil second-guesses and all. Each is typed out beside the scan, so we can actually cook from them.

The oldest card in the box: Smaa Brød, from Inga Beate Falde, 1955

40 cards so far, from cakes to a curing brine to one that turned out not to be food at all.

Adding to this

This is a keeping place, not a finished thing. If you have a card, a photograph of one, or the story that goes with a recipe (who made it, who it was for, the year it went wrong), send it along and it gets added here. Stories will live beside the recipes once there are some to tell.

A photograph taken on a phone is plenty. The cards are small and the writing is faint, so more light beats more megapixels.
